About us

A small studio making web work that lasts.

We started Pebble in 2018 because we were tired of websites that broke six months after launch. We wanted to make sites you'd want to keep around — fast, durable, and quietly beautiful.

Today we're a team of three working with clients across publishing, hardware, and quiet luxury. We take on a handful of projects a year and care deeply about each one.

If you're looking for someone to ship a hundred-page marketing site in six weeks, that's not us. If you're looking for someone to make one page exceptional, hello.

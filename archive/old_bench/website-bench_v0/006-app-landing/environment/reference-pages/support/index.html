Help & frequently asked questions.

Quick answers to the things people ask us most.

1
Is Loop really free to use?

Yes — the Starter plan is free forever and includes everything you need to track up to five habits. Premium just adds unlimited habits and deeper insights.

2
Does Loop work without an internet connection?

Absolutely. Loop runs fully offline on your device. Cloud sync is optional and only activates if you're on a Premium plan and choose to enable it.

3
What happens to my streak if I miss a day?

You get one free "rest day" per week so a single off-day won't break your streak. We believe consistency matters more than perfection.

4
Can I sync between my phone and tablet?

Yes, Premium users can sync seamlessly across iOS and Android devices using their Loop account. Your data is end-to-end encrypted in transit.

5
How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your phone's subscription settings — no emails, no hassle. You'll keep Premium features until the end of your billing cycle.

Pricing Questions

The short answers most teams ask before signing up.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrades take effect instantly, and downgrades apply at the next billing cycle. Your voice profiles and drafts always migrate with you.

Is the 14-day trial really free?

It is. No credit card needed for Spark, and Momentum trials don't auto-charge until day 15 — you'll get a reminder email 48 hours before.

How does Orbit per-seat billing work?

Each active member is one seat at $79/mo. Invite, deactivate, and reassign freely — we prorate to the day, so you only pay for time used.

Do you offer nonprofit or edu discounts?

Yes. Verified nonprofits, classrooms, and student journalists receive 40% off Momentum. Reach out via the Get Started page to apply.

How Noctua Works

Three simple steps between you and the most detailed sleep report you've ever read. No wearables, no straps — just your phone and our sonar engine.

01

Place Your Phone

Set your phone face-down on the nightstand within arm's reach of the bed. Plug it in, open Noctua, and tap "Start Night."

02

Noctua Listens

Inaudible sonar pulses measure your movement and breathing through the night. Your microphone never records audible sound or speech.

03

Wake to Insights

A smart alarm rouses you in light sleep within your chosen window. Your full sleep report, cycle breakdown, and coaching tips load instantly.

The science behind sonar sleep tracking

📡

How sonar motion detection works. Noctua emits ultra-high-frequency tones — far above human hearing — through your phone's speaker. The microphone captures how those tones bounce off your chest as you breathe and shift. By analyzing tiny shifts in the returning waveform (a technique called Doppler analysis), Noctua maps your respiratory rate and gross body movement with millimeter precision, all without touching you.

Every minute of that signal is processed locally on your device and translated into sleep stages: awake, light, deep, and REM. We never upload raw audio, and your nightly report is built from movement signatures alone. The result is wearable-grade accuracy from a device you already own — and a tracker that disappears the moment you wake up.

Origin & Mission

A coalition built in Pittsburgh, for the nation's makers.

The Ironclad Futures Summit was founded in 2019 by the Pittsburgh Manufacturing Coalition — a working group of regional fabricators, university researchers, and policy advisors who believed that the future of domestic production deserved a serious, in-person forum. What began as a single-day roundtable inside a converted steelworks has, in six years, become the Northeast's most rigorous gathering for industrial technology.

Today, the Summit convenes engineers, lawmakers, and investors across two intensive days each October. Our mission is unchanged: to surface the practical investments, policy levers, and engineering breakthroughs that will keep advanced manufacturing rooted on American soil — and to do it through frank, evidence-driven dialogue rather than keynote spectacle.

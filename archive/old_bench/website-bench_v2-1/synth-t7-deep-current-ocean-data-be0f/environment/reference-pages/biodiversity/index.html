Chapter IV — Phylogenetic Wheel

The Marine Tree of Life

The oceans host an estimated 240,000 described species — and perhaps ten times that many awaiting discovery. This radial taxonomy traces the six great branches of marine life, from the photosynthetic plankton that anchor every food web to the cetaceans that range from pole to pole.

Reading the wheel

Six taxonomic branches radiate from a central node. Leaf color alternates to aid scanning; the displayed counts reflect formally described extant species as catalogued by WoRMS, 2023.

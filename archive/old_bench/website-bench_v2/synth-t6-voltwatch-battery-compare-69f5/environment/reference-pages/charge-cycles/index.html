Charge Cycles & Calendar Life

Projected cycle counts and calendar life for all 14 catalog batteries at three industry-standard depth-of-discharge (DoD) levels. Lower DoD operation extends usable cycles substantially — particularly for LFP and solid-state chemistries. Cells exceeding 3,000 cycles at 80% DoD are flagged as best-in-class.

The Signature Collection

Three series, each born of fire & cooled into form.

Our home collection distills twenty-six years of commissioned practice into limited-run editions — vessels, panels, and sculptural columns hand-blown in the Portland studio.

Edition 01 · Vessels

Solstice Series

Amber & Gold Blown Vessels

Hand-blown in honeyed amber and inclusions of 23k gold leaf, the Solstice vessels capture the long, slow light of late afternoon. Each piece is shaped on the iron in a single pull, then annealed for forty-eight hours.

Available in three silhouettes — Reed, Orb, and Carafe — sold individually or as a curated triptych for mantel and shelf compositions.

  • Heights 180–420 mm
  • Edition of 60
  • Signed & numbered
  • Lead time 6–8 weeks

Edition 02 · Panels

Arctic Shelf

Clear & Ice-Blue Panel Work

A modular system of cast architectural panels in optically clear crystal layered with veins of pale cerulean. Designed to filter daylight across partitions, stair walls, and entry vestibules without obscuring volume.

Panels interlock along a 600mm grid and can be specified in flat, fluted, or fractured surface treatments to suit project acoustics and sightlines.

  • 600 × 1200 mm modules
  • 22 mm cast thickness
  • Three surface finishes
  • Trade pricing available

Edition 03 · Sculpture

Ember Column

Freestanding Sculptural Glass

A floor-standing column of stacked, hand-pulled glass discs graduating from carbon black at the base to molten amber at the crown. Internally lit by a concealed warm-LED core that pools light through every translucent layer.

Commissioned in three heights, the Ember Column anchors lobbies, gallery niches, and double-height residential staircases with quiet, radiant presence.

  • 1.4 m, 1.8 m, 2.4 m
  • Bronze patinated base
  • Integrated dimmer
  • Made to order

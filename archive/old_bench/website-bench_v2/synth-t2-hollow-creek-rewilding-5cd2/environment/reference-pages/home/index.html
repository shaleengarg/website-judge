MILLER'S BEND

Sedge Grass Returns to Miller's Bend

After three seasons of careful seeding and the removal of stubborn fescue mats, native carex once again lines the lower bend of Finley Creek. Volunteers from the Seed Harvest Team report watching young sandpipers nesting in cover that didn't exist two summers ago — a small but unmistakable signal of returning balance.

SCHOOLYARD

A Prairie Grows Behind Rockbridge Elementary

Last fall, fifty-six fourth graders planted little bluestem and butterfly milkweed in a quarter-acre patch that had been mowed turf since 1998. By June, monarchs were laying eggs on the new stems. The Schoolyard Habitat Grants program now supports twelve partner schools across three counties.

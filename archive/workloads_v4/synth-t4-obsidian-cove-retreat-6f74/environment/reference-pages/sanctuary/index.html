Clifftop Location

Perched 80 metres above the Aegean on a black volcanic promontory, every window frames an unbroken horizon — sea, sky, and the distant lanterns of Naxos at dusk.

Stone, salt, and silence.

Obsidian Cove was conceived as the antidote to the modern resort — no lobbies, no lines, no rush. Twenty-four suites, twelve metres of cliff between each, and a single sweeping path down to the water.

Our staff outnumber guests two to one. Our chef sources from six island farms within twenty kilometres. And every morning, the sea is waiting.

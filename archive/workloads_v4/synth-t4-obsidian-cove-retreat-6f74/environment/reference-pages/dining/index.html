Sunrise Terrace Breakfast

Served two hundred metres above the Aegean, where the first light strikes the cliff face. A leisurely ritual of stone-ground coffee, sweet pastry, and silent horizon.

C
The Cup

Single-Origin Coffee

Beans roasted weekly in Athens, brewed three ways — slow filter, traditional briki, or chilled cold-extraction. Paired with cardamom milk or first-press olive oil for those new to the ritual.

P
The Plate

Stone Oven Pastry

Bougatsa filled with vanilla custard, sesame-honey koulouri, fig & thyme galette, and our daily sourdough served warm with kasseri butter and wildflower honey from the lighthouse hives.

V
The View

270° of Open Sea

Tables are reserved by sunrise direction. Expect distant ferry silhouettes, the occasional Eleonora's falcon, and a long unbroken horizon — the only interruption is the bell announcing fresh bread from the oven.

The Cellar at Obsidian Cove

Eight Hundred Bottles, One Stone Cellar

Hewn from the same volcanic rock as the grotto itself, our cellar holds a curated selection of Aegean and continental wines kept at a natural 14°C year-round. Private tastings are hosted nightly by our sommelier, Dimitra Kontou.

Ethos

We build identities that hold their breath.

Obsidian Fold is a three-person studio working between cultural institutions and independent luxury labels. We treat identity as architecture — load-bearing, deliberate, slow. Each system we ship is engineered to outlast trend cycles, refusing decoration in favour of geometry, restraint, and the precision of a well-folded sheet of paper.

The Three.

Principals · Partners · Practice Leads

House Rules
— 04
01

Three clients per quarter, no more.

Scarcity is not a tactic; it is the only condition under which we can defend the work.

02

No pitches. No spec work.

We begin with a paid discovery week. Either we earn the engagement or we part on good terms.

03

Custom type by default.

Every system ships with at least one drawn glyph form. Identity that cannot be set in metal cannot be set at all.

04

The studio answers its own phone.

Account management is a role we have refused since 2017. You speak to the people drawing the marks.

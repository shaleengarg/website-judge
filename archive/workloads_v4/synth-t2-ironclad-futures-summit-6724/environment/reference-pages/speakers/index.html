Speakers

Meet the operations leaders, logistics engineers, and policy voices shaping the next era of North American industrial resilience. The 2025 program features twelve confirmed speakers across three days in Pittsburgh.

Keynote Speakers

MR
KEYNOTE
Dr. Margaret Reyes
Chief Resilience Officer, U.S. Manufacturing Consortium

Dr. Reyes leads federal industrial resilience strategy across 14 priority supply corridors. Her work redefined the national framework for critical-component sourcing after 2022.

JT
KEYNOTE
James Tolliver
CEO, Cascadia Freight Systems

James oversees one of the largest intermodal networks in the Pacific Northwest, moving more than 9 million tons annually. He is a frequent advisor to congressional logistics committees.

Featured Speakers

AN
Aisha Nakamura
Director of Global Logistics at Ferrocept Industries

Aisha has rebuilt Ferrocept's transcontinental rail-to-port pipeline over the last four years. She regularly publishes on multi-modal redundancy planning.

BC
Brendan Cho
Senior Policy Analyst at the Allegheny Manufacturing Council

Brendan authored the council's 2024 white paper on reshoring incentives for mid-tier metal fabricators. He briefs state legislators monthly on workforce policy.

SP
Sofia Patel
VP of Operations at Keystone Steelworks

Sofia runs three mills across western Pennsylvania producing structural steel for infrastructure clients. She championed Keystone's transition to predictive maintenance in 2023.

DO
Daniel Okafor
Head of Supply Chain at Northbridge Robotics

Daniel manages component sourcing across 22 countries for industrial automation systems. His team pioneered dual-source contracts for semiconductor-grade actuators.

EM
Elena Marchetti
Principal Engineer at Rust Belt Energy Solutions

Elena designs grid-scale energy systems for retrofitted manufacturing campuses. She holds nine patents in distributed industrial power balancing.

RW
Robert Whitfield
Federal Liaison, North American Ports Alliance

Robert coordinates infrastructure policy among 38 member ports along the eastern seaboard. He testified before the Senate Commerce Committee in 2024.

LH
Linh Hoang
Director of Procurement at Monongahela Materials

Linh transformed Monongahela's tier-2 supplier vetting process, cutting onboarding from 90 to 14 days. She mentors emerging procurement professionals in the region.

GS
Gregory Sandoval
Logistics Architect at Beacon Cargo Networks

Gregory designs cross-border freight routing systems serving more than 400 industrial clients. He led Beacon's response to the 2023 Midwest rail disruptions.

PK
Priya Krishnan
Research Fellow at Carnegie Industrial Policy Institute

Priya studies the intersection of trade policy and regional manufacturing capacity. Her recent book examines four decades of Rust Belt reinvention.

MF
Marcus Feldman
COO at Three Rivers Chemical Holdings

Marcus oversees safety, throughput, and regulatory compliance across six chemical plants. He chairs the regional industrial mutual-aid committee.

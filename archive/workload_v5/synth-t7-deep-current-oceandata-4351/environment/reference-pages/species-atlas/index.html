Species Atlas of the Global Ocean

47 biodiversity hotspots · 12 featured taxa · 8 marine phyla mapped

The atlas aggregates IUCN Red List assessments, IPBES range models, and OBIS occurrence records into a single navigable map. Hotspot markers are scaled to the local Shannon biodiversity index H' and tinted along a teal→coral axis to indicate aggregate conservation status. Featured species in the side panel are drawn from the IUCN Top‑12 priority list for FY2024.

Atlas alert · 41% of featured taxa show declining trend
Of the 247k currently described marine species, 41% of the IUCN priority-12 sample exhibit a downward population trajectory over the last decade. Cetaceans and chondrichthyans remain disproportionately affected by industrial fishing pressure.

Pledge Adoption · 2024

What "30×30" actually means

Protect at least 30% of land and ocean by 2030, with effective management of biodiversity hotspots. Today, only 2.9% of the ocean is fully protected from extractive activity — the rest is paper-protected at best.

Window is closing

At current ratification rates, the High Seas Treaty enters force in late 2025. Coastal states must ratify before COP30 to keep the 30×30 timeline credible.

The data does not need our optimism. It needs our action — measured, repeated, and shared. — Dr. Annaliese Cordero, Editor-in-chief · Deep Current

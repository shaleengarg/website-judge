How it works

From draft to dispatch in three steps

Luminos plugs into the tools your product team already lives in. Connect once, then turn raw tickets and Notion scratchpads into release-ready copy without leaving your flow.

1

Connect your workspace

OAuth into Notion, Jira, Linear, or GitHub in under 30 seconds. Read-only by default — Luminos never edits source documents.

~30 sec
2

Pick a doc or ticket

Choose a single Jira epic, a Notion page, or batch-select a sprint's worth of issues. Pick your output: release note, changelog, or email.

1 click
3

Review and publish

Tune the tone, edit inline, and push straight to Confluence, Intercom, or your changelog page. Drafts stay versioned forever.

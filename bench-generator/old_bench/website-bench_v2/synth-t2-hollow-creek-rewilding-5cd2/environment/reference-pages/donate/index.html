Your Gift Restores What Was Lost

Every contribution returns native life to the Ozark plateau. $35 plants 50 native seedlings along eroded creek banks, and $150 funds one full acre of invasive species removal in priority restoration zones.

Our Mission

Hollow Creek Rewilding restores native grasslands and riparian corridors across the Ozark plateau through volunteer stewardship, seed banking, and community science.

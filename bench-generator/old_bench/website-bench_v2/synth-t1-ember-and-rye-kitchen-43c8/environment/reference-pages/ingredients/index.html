Recipe · Mise en Place Serves 4

Ingredients

Gather everything before the sear. The braise and the glaze are prepped separately and brought together at the very end.

For the Braise

  • 4 lbs bone-in beef short ribs, English-cut
  • 3 cups rich beef stock
  • 2 tbsp smoked paprika (sweet, not hot)
  • 6 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 3 medium shallots, halved lengthwise
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 6 sprigs fresh thyme

For the Rye Glaze

  • ½ cup rye whiskey
  • 3 tbsp dark brown sugar, packed
  • 1 tbsp whole-grain mustard
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

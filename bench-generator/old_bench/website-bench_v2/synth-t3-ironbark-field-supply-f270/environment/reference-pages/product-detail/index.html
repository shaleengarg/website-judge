About this axe

Forged by hand at the Gransfors Bruks smithy in Bergsjö, Sweden, the Small Forest Axe carries the initials of the smith who hammered its head. Its lean profile and grain-aligned hickory handle make it a trusted companion for limbing felled trees, splitting kindling, and shaping camp timber where a full felling axe is more than the job needs.

The convex edge is ground for clean biting cuts and easy maintenance in the field. Each axe arrives sharp, hung, and oiled, paired with a vegetable-tanned grain-leather sheath. Built for serious daily use by rangers, smallholders, and bushcrafters who value tools that outlast the person swinging them.

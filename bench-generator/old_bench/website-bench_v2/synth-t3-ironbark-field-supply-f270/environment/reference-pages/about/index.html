About Ironbark Field Supply

Hard tools for hard country. Built for farmers, rangers, and bushcraft professionals across Australia and beyond.

Our Origin

Founded2011 HeadquartersTamworth, NSW FounderPetra Halvorsen Team14 staff

Ironbark Field Supply was founded in 2011 in Tamworth, New South Wales, by Petra Halvorsen — a former land-clearing contractor who spent fifteen years running crews through fire-prone scrub in the New England tablelands. After years of watching cheap imported tools fail at the worst possible moments — split helves on the third day, gummed-up saws by morning smoko, fire kits that wouldn't strike in the wet — she decided to start importing and curating the gear her own crews actually trusted.

PH
Petra Halvorsen
Founder & Head Buyer

The first catalogue was twelve products mailed out of a shed off the New England Highway. Today we ship more than four hundred SKUs nationwide, but the buying philosophy hasn't shifted: every item is field-tested by working contractors before it reaches the storefront, and nothing makes it onto the shelf unless one of our staff would carry it themselves into a day's work.

Core Values

DURABLE

Built to Last

We stock tools rated for daily professional use, not weekend kits dressed up to look the part. If a product can't survive a fortnight of fence-line work, it doesn't earn a place in our catalogue.

SOURCED

Expert-Sourced

Every brand we carry has been vetted by working rangers, foresters, or land-management contractors on our buying panel. We visit forges and workshops directly rather than relying on distributor spec sheets.

FAIR

No Nonsense Returns

If a tool fails under reasonable working conditions inside 90 days, we replace it without a questionnaire. We'd rather lose a sale than ship you back into the bush with gear we can't stand behind.

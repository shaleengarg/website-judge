04 — Process

Four moves.
One argument.

Every brand we build travels the same diagonal: from quiet attention to public consequence. We don't sprint, and we don't stall — we proceed at 45 degrees, with intent.

The Diagonal Method

Phases 01 — 04
— 01 / Listen

LISTEN

We begin in silence. Founder interviews, archival audits, market reconnaissance — every claim a client makes about themselves is recorded before we offer a single line of opinion. The first deliverable is a transcript, not a moodboard.

Weeks 01 — 02
02 / Excavate —

EXCAVATE

We dig through the language, the legacy, the contradictions. What's load-bearing? What's decoration that's been mistaken for structure? Excavation produces a single-page brand thesis — the argument the identity will be built to defend.

Weeks 03 — 05
— 03 / Build

BUILD

Mark, system, voice, motion — constructed against a 6-unit modular grid and pressure-tested across the real surfaces the brand will live on. We deliver fewer rounds with more conviction. Every revision is documented as a reasoned argument, not a preference.

Weeks 06 — 11
04 / Release —

RELEASE

Launch is not a finish line; it's a posture the brand has to hold. We script the rollout, train internal teams, and stay attached for ninety days of public life — so the system bends without breaking. Then we let go.

Weeks 12 — 16+
We don't decorate.
We define.

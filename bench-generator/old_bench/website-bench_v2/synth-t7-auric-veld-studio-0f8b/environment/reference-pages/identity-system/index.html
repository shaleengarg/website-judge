03 — The AV Construction

A mark built on six units.

Two rhombuses, one shared edge.

The Auric Veld mark is not a logo so much as a thesis: that two opposing forms — angular and warm, structural and emotional — can share a single edge without collapsing into one another. The left rhombus carries the A; the right carries the V. Their kiss is the studio.

Every dimension is locked to a six-unit modular grid. Width, height, and the inner negative space are integer multiples of one unit (66.6px at scale). This is the same grid we deploy across every client identity we ship.

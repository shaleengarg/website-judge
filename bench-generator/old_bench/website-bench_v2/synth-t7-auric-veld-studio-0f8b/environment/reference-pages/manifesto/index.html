Three Claims We Stake
/ 01

Brand is posture.

How a company stands before the world precedes anything it says. We work on the spine first, the surface second.

/ 02

Identity is argument.

Every logo is a thesis, every palette a position. Marks that don't take sides cannot be defended — or remembered.

/ 03

Design is consequence.

What we build leaves a residue: in a market, in a memory, in a mood. We design knowing the world keeps the receipt.

A Closing Note

We design the spine of a company, then let everything else hang from it — colour, language, gesture, room.

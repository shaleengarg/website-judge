The Crevasse Kitchen

Pacific Northwest terroir served at the edge of the basalt cliffs. Every plate is shaped by what the tide returns and the forest gives — foraged, fermented, and finished over open coastal fire.

The Tide Tasting

Seatings 6:30 & 8:45 PM

A seven-act narrative built from the morning's foraged ingredients — sea beans pulled from the bluff at dawn, halibut line-caught off Cape Foulweather, and madrone-smoked finishes that arrive under cloche to reveal driftwood embers.

Raw Bar
Cliffside Counter · 12 Seats

The Obsidian Raw Bar

Open 4:00 – 11:00 PM

An intimate twelve-seat counter carved from a single slab of cooled lava rock, facing the Pacific through floor-to-ceiling glass. Oysters are shucked to order from four Oregon estuaries while the staff pours rare grower champagnes and aquavits aged in driftwood casks.

  • Netarts Bay oysters, finger lime mignonetteBy the half-dozen
  • Albacore crudo, green strawberry, shisoDaily catch
  • Spot prawn tartare, fermented chiliSeasonal
  • Sea urchin toast, cultured butter, noriSignature

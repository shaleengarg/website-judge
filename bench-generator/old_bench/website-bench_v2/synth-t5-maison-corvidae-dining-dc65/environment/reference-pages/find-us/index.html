Nous Trouver

418 NW Kearney Street
Portland · Oregon · 97209
Pearl District — between 13th & 14th

The townhouse stands midway down the block, set back from its neighbors behind a wrought-iron gate and a single brass door. There is no plate, no awning, no menu in the window — only a numbered transom and the soft amber of the vestibule beyond.

Ring the bell once, firmly, and wait. A member of the house will receive you in the interior vestibule, where coats are taken and the evening begins. We ask that arrivals be made between 7:15 and 7:30; the dining room is sealed at the first course.

Parking & Transit

Valet service is not offered. The Pearl District public garage at NW 13th & Everett is a three-minute walk from our door and accommodates evening guests at a flat rate after 6 PM. For those arriving by TriMet, the NW 11th & Glisan streetcar stop lies one block south, and the MAX Blue and Red lines serve Galleria/SW 10th, a short cab fare away.

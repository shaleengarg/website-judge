Réservations

Maison Corvidae seats one turn each evening, at half past seven, Wednesday through Sunday. The dining room holds twenty-eight guests, and the kitchen begins service for all tables simultaneously; we therefore ask that you arrive no earlier than seven fifteen, and no later than seven twenty-five, so that the first course may reach every seat at its intended temperature.

Parties are limited to six guests. Larger gatherings cannot be accommodated within the contours of our menu or the architecture of our small townhouse; we trust you will understand. A per-person deposit of seventy-five dollars is collected at the time of booking and applied in full to your final bill. Cancellations made more than seventy-two hours in advance are refunded without question; thereafter, the deposit is retained.

Reservations open on the first day of each month for the month that follows. We answer the telephone personally between two and five in the afternoon, Tuesday through Saturday; the inbox is read each morning. Should both lines be quiet, leave a name and a number, and we will return your call before the evening's first pour.

Walk-ins are never accommodated. The door remains closed to guests without a confirmed booking.

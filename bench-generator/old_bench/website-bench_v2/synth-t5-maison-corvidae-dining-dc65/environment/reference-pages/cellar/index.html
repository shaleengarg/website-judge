The Wine Program

Sommelier Théodore Vance has built the cellar over seven years of patient acquisition, favoring small négociants and domaine bottlings from the Côte d'Or alongside a quiet thread of Pacific Northwest producers whose work speaks the same dialect of soil and restraint. The list is short by design — never more than ninety labels — and every bottle is chosen to converse with a course on the evening's menu.

Pairings may be selected à la carte or as a guided seven-glass flight. Théodore receives guests at the cellar table before service each evening at seven.

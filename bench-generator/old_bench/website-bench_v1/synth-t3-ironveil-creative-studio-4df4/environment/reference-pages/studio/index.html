§ 03 — The Studio

We build brands
for the unreasonable.

Ironveil is a fourteen-person studio in Brooklyn working with founders and operators who have decided the category they're entering deserves better than its current vocabulary. We don't service incumbents. We sharpen challengers — the second-act companies, the spinouts, the people building the thing that was supposed to be impossible.

Our practice is split across strategy, identity, and digital, but the seam between them is intentionally thin. A naming exercise leaks into a typography decision; a positioning sprint sets the tone for a launch film. We believe the worst brand work is the work that respects departmental boundaries more than it respects the audience.

We are deliberately small, deliberately senior, and deliberately picky. Eleven years in, we still take fewer than a dozen engagements a year, and we still answer our own email. If that sounds like a constraint, it is — and it's the one we're least willing to negotiate.

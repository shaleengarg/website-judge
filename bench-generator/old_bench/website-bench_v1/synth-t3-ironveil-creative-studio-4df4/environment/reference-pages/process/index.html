A five-phase engagement model refined over 11 years of challenger-brand work. Each phase concludes with a signed-off deliverable.
How We Work — Process No. 05

From quiet research
to public launch.

Ironveil engagements run on a deliberate cadence: we resist the urge to rush to artifact, and we resist the urge to over-plan. Every phase exists to make the next one sharper.

01Phase

Discovery

Brand Week 1 — Week 2

We embed with stakeholders, audit existing materials, and map the competitive territory. The goal is to surface tensions, not solutions — by the end of week two we know what nobody is willing to say out loud.

Deliverables
  • Stakeholder interviews (8–12 sessions)
  • Brand & market audit deck
  • Category tension map
  • Discovery readout, in person
02Phase

Strategy

Brand Digital Week 3 — Week 5

We translate discovery into a written platform: positioning, audience, voice, and the strategic bet the brand is making. Nothing is designed in this phase — we earn the visual work by sharpening the words first.

Deliverables
  • Positioning statement & rationale
  • Audience & segmentation brief
  • Brand narrative + messaging pillars
  • Verbal identity guidelines (v1)
03Phase

Concept

Brand Print Week 6 — Week 9

Two or three distinct visual territories are developed in parallel, each anchored to the strategy. We present them as full worlds — logo, type, color, art direction — so the choice is about direction, not detail.

Deliverables
  • 2–3 identity territories, fully dressed
  • Logo system + wordmark studies
  • Typographic specimen + color trials
  • Sample applications (print + screen)
04Phase

Refine

Brand Digital Week 10 — Week 13

The chosen direction is hardened into a working system: components, edge cases, motion, and documentation. We test the identity against the messiest real-world use cases we can invent before it ships.

Deliverables
  • Final logo + lockup suite
  • Brand guidelines document (PDF + web)
  • Digital component library
  • Motion principles & key animations
05Phase

Launch

Digital Print Week 14 — Week 16

We support the rollout across the channels that matter — site, social, press, internal — and stay close for 30 days post-launch. Success is measured against the bet defined in Strategy, not against applause.

Deliverables
  • Launch website + asset library
  • Internal launch kit & FAQ
  • Press & partner toolkit
  • 30-day post-launch review

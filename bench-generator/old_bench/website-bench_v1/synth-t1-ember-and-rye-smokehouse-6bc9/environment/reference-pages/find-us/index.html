Find Us

4812 Cinder Road
Amarillo, TX 79101

We sit on the south side of Cinder Road in a low, tin-roofed building with smoke rising out the back lot from sunup most days. Free gravel parking wraps around the building; overflow lot is across the alley behind the feed store. Look for the old water tower two blocks east — if you can see it, you're close.

Getting Here

Nearest cross street: Cinder Road at South 48th Avenue, one block west of the rail crossing.

Nearest bus stop: Amarillo Transit Route 14, stop #212 — Cinder & 49th, a two-minute walk east of the smokehouse door.

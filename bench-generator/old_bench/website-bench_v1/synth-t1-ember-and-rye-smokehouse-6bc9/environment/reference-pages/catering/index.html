Catering

We Bring The Pit

Ember & Rye handles off-site events for 20 to 300 guests, hauling our full pit setup wherever the work goes — backyard weddings, ranch reunions, company barbecues, wakes, christenings, and anything else that calls for honest smoke and a long table.

We arrive the night before, light the oak, and feed your people hot off the fire. No chafing dishes pretending to be lunch. No shortcuts. Just brisket, ribs, and sausage cooked the only way we know how.

Getting There

Parking

A gated surface lot sits directly adjacent to the building on Meldrum Street with roughly 90 spaces, free for registered attendees on a first-come basis. Overflow street parking is unmetered after 6:00 PM the night before and freely available on the morning of the event.

Public Transit

DDOT Bus Line 16 (Dexter) stops one block north at Meldrum & Vernor, running every 20 minutes from downtown. The nearest QLine streetcar stop is Grand Boulevard, a nine-minute walk east through Corktown — signage along the route will be marked in amber the morning of the summit.

Accessibility

The main floor is single-level with a zero-step entry off Meldrum Street. Two ADA-compliant restrooms, accessible seating in every session block, and reserved front-row spaces are available without request. A quiet decompression room is open all day on the mezzanine, reachable by elevator.

The Pitch

Ironbell is a one-day, invite-open conference for founders less than five years into the work — no sponsors, no demo booths, no keynote theater.

 

We meet in a converted machine shop in Corktown for eight hours of plain talk: what's hard, what's working, what isn't. Eight sessions, six operators on stage, two hundred seats in the room.

 

No fluff. No fees. Bring a notebook.

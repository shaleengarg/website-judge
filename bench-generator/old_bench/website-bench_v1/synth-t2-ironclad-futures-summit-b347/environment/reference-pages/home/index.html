Conference Themes

Three Pillars. One Industrial Future.

Across three intensive days, IRONCLAD convenes engineers, operators, and researchers around the technologies reshaping how things are made, moved, and powered.

⚙️

Advanced Materials

From metal additive manufacturing to engineered composites, explore the substances redefining what's structurally possible. Sessions examine production scaling, supply resilience, and lab-to-floor pathways.

Track A
🏭

Smart Factories

Industry 4.0 has matured — and so have the integration challenges. Hear how leading plants are unifying robotics, MES platforms, edge AI, and human operators on a single connected floor.

Track B
⚡

Sustainable Energy

Decarbonization is no longer optional for heavy industry. Track sessions cover green hydrogen, electrified process heat, grid-interactive plants, and the financial case for clean retrofits.

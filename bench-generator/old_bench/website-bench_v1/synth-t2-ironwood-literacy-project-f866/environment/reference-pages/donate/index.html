Your Gift Opens a Book

Every dollar donated to the Ironwood Literacy Project goes directly toward putting trained tutors, free workbooks, and welcoming classroom space in the hands of adult learners across rural Appalachia. We keep our overhead lean — fewer than 9 cents of every dollar covers administration — so your generosity reaches the kitchen tables, library back rooms, and church basements where the real work happens.

Whether you give once or commit to monthly support, you become part of a quiet movement that believes literacy is the foundation of dignity, employment, and family stability. Choose a level that fits, or design your own gift. Either way, thank you.

About

The Ironwood Literacy Project provides free adult reading programs across the hollers and small towns of rural Appalachia. Reading changes everything — one adult at a time.

Field Notes

Brief dispatches from the trail — unedited, written where they happened.

14 October 2024
Chamonix Valley, France

Glacier louder than expected today. A low groaning under the ice that you feel before you hear it. Bought black bread and hard cheese from a shop with no sign on the door.

02 September 2024
Sarek National Park, Sweden

Five days without seeing another person. The reindeer pay no attention. Sky stayed the color of wet tin from dawn until something that could be called dusk.

19 July 2024
Aoraki / Mount Cook, New Zealand

Wind shifted at 3 a.m. and stayed shifted. Climbed half a pitch and turned around — the right decision, which always feels worse than it should. Tea afterwards in the hut, three of us not talking.

06 June 2024
Ladakh, Northern India

The air at 4,800m is honest in a way I had forgotten. Slept badly, woke clear-headed. Passed a monk on a motorbike carrying a length of corrugated roofing.

22 April 2024
Picos de Europa, Spain

Limestone like white bone in the morning. Found a cave entrance not on the map and didn't go in, which I am still thinking about. Coffee in Potes after, dark and unkind.

11 March 2024
Lofoten Islands, Norway

Stood on a beach at midnight with snow falling sideways into the sea. No metaphor reached me. That itself was the note worth keeping.

28 January 2024
Cordillera Blanca, Peru

Acclimatising slowly. A boy at the hostel taught me a card game with rules he kept revising. I lost cheerfully. The mountains here look painted on.

09 November 2023
Isle of Skye, Scotland

Rain for three days then a single hour of light so sharp it hurt to look at the ridgeline. Wrote nothing. Walked twenty-two kilometres and ate two dinners.

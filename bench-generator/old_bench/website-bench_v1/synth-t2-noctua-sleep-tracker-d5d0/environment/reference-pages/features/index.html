Features

Everything your sleep deserves.

Six powerful tools working quietly through the night — so you wake up rested, informed, and ready.

🌙

REM Phase Detection

  • Identifies light, deep, and REM cycles with 94% accuracy
  • Visualizes hypnograms for every night in your timeline
  • Flags interrupted REM that may impact next-day focus
🔊

Noise Environment Scoring

  • Detects snoring, traffic, and ambient room disturbances
  • Generates a nightly quiet-score from 0 to 100
  • Saves short audio clips for review the next morning
❤️

Heart Rate Sync

  • Connects with Apple Watch, Fitbit, and Garmin devices
  • Overlays resting heart rate trends on sleep charts
  • Alerts you to unusual nocturnal BPM patterns
⏰

Bedtime Reminders

  • Adaptive nudges based on your real sleep debt
  • Gentle wind-down sequence 45 minutes before lights out
  • Syncs with calendar to anticipate early mornings
📊

Sleep Debt Tracker

  • Rolling 14-day deficit calculation in hours and minutes
  • Personalized recovery plan to repay missed rest
  • Weekly trend reports delivered every Sunday
👥

Partner Mode

  • Tracks two sleepers in the same bed independently
  • Compares cycles to find ideal shared wake windows
  • Private dashboards — your data stays your own

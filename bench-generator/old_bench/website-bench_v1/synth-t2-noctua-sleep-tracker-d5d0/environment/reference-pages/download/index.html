Frequently Asked Questions

Does Noctua need a wearable device?
No. Noctua uses your phone's microphone and accelerometer to detect movement and breathing patterns — no watch or band required.
Will the app drain my battery overnight?
Average overnight drain is under 8% when the phone is plugged in or in low-power mode. Most users sleep with the charger connected.
Is my sleep data shared or sold?
Never. All recordings are processed on-device and deleted by morning. Aggregate, anonymized metrics stay on your account.
Can I cancel Plus or Family anytime?
Yes — cancel from your App Store or Google Play subscription settings. You keep premium access through the end of the billing period.

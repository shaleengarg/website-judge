The Tasting Menu

Autumn
Progression

October 2024 · Six Courses

Each evening, the hearth is lit at four o'clock. By seven, oak and apple-wood embers have settled into a slow, glowing bed — the foundation for every plate that follows. The progression below changes with the harvest and the weather. What arrives at the table is what the mountains, the rivers, and our friends in the fields have offered this week.

The Courses

  1. Course One · Amuse
    Smoked Trout Roe on Buckwheat Sablé
    Cultured cream, charred leek ash, pickled ramp, dill flower
    Sunburst Trout Farms · Canton, NC
  2. Course Two · First
    Hearth-Roasted Beets & Sorghum
    Whipped goat curd, sumac, toasted pumpkin seed, burnt honey, sorrel
    Ivy Creek Family Farm · Barnardsville, NC
  3. Course Three · Second
    Cairn Springs Wheat Berry Porridge
    Cultured butter, roasted celeriac, brown-butter chanterelles, sage
    Cairn Springs Mill · Burlington, WA
  4. Course Four · Fish
    Ember-Cooked Rainbow Trout
    Brown-butter dashi, apple cider gastrique, charred cabbage, salt-cured egg yolk
    Sunburst Trout Farms · Canton, NC
  5. Course Five · Main
    Forty-Day Aged Beef over Oak Coals
    Bone-marrow jus, smoked turnip, fermented black walnut, watercress
    Hickory Nut Gap Farm · Fairview, NC
  6. Course Six · Dessert
    Burnt Cream with Wood-Fired Pear
    Buckwheat honey, brûléed muscovado, candied chestnut, crème fraîche ice
    Creasman Farms · Hendersonville, NC

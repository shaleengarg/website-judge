Reservations

Reserve Your Evening

A single seating welcomes sixty guests each night to the hearth. We hold the room for you from the moment you arrive until the last pour of the evening — there is no rush, and there is no second turn.

Service

Evenings

Wednesday through Sunday

The dining room is closed Monday and Tuesday so the kitchen may rest, develop, and forage. We do not serve lunch.

Seating

A single seating at 7:00 PM

Guests are encouraged to arrive between 6:30 and 6:45 PM for an aperitif at the hearth. The six-course progression begins promptly at seven.

Party Size

Two to eight guests

Parties of five or more are seated at our communal oak table, milled from a single felled chestnut. Larger gatherings may inquire about the Hearth Room — see private dining below.

Policies

Deposit

$50 per guest at the time of booking

The deposit is credited in full toward your final bill on the evening of your reservation. We accept all major cards.

Cancellation

Forty-eight hours, with our thanks

Cancellations or reductions in party size made within 48 hours of the seating forfeit the deposit. We extend this courtesy because the menu is composed, plated, and paced for the precise count we hold for you.

Dietary Accommodations

Kindly notify us 72 hours in advance

Allergies, intolerances, and abstentions can almost always be honored with proper notice. The menu is wood-fire driven and grain-forward; pescatarian and vegetarian progressions are available.

Attire

Considered and unhurried

There is no formal dress code. The mill is warm in winter and cool in summer; a jacket or shawl is often welcome.

Private Dining

The Hearth Room, situated above the original millstone foundation, accommodates seated parties of ten to twenty-two for a bespoke menu developed in conversation with the chef. Buyouts of the full dining room are considered for select dates each season.

For inquiries regarding rehearsal dinners, milestone gatherings, vintner evenings, and corporate hosting, please write to us directly. We typically respond within two business days.

Booking

Reservations open on the first day of each calendar month for the month following. To hold a table, please call the host desk between 2:00 and 10:00 PM, Tuesday through Sunday, at (828) 554-0917. A confirmation, the deposit receipt, and arrival instructions for the unmarked gravel entrance will follow by post or by email at your preference.

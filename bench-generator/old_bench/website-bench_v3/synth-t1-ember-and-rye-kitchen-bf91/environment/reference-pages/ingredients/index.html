Ingredients

Everything you'll need for the braise and the glossy rye finish. Measure before you sear.

For the Short Ribs

  • 2.5 lb bone-in beef short ribs
  • 1.5 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp coarse-ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp rendered beef tallow (or neutral oil)
  • 1 large yellow onion, rough-chopped
  • 2 medium carrots, cut into thick coins
  • 3 stalks celery, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 6 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef bone broth
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp whole black peppercorns

For the Rye Glaze

  • 3 tbsp smoked rye whiskey
  • 2 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp sorghum molasses
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 cup reserved braising liquid
  • 1 tbsp cold unsalted butter
  • to finish flaky sea salt

Slow-Braised Short Rib with Smoked Rye Whiskey Glaze

Born in a low-ceilinged ranch kitchen outside Sheridan, Wyoming, this dish is what we cooked the night the first hard frost split the cottonwoods. Bone-in short ribs are coaxed for hours over a slow flame, then finished with a glaze of smoked rye whiskey, dark molasses, and pan drippings — a recipe that tastes of woodsmoke, patience, and the kind of cold that settles into the floorboards.

Prep Time 30 min
Cook Time 3 hr 45 min
Serves 4

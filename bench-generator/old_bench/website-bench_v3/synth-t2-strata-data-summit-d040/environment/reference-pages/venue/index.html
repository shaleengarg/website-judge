The Venue

Two days of keynotes, workshops, and conversations at the Pittsburgh Convention Center — at the confluence of three rivers and the heart of the city's tech corridor.

Pittsburgh Convention Center

1000 Fort Duquesne Blvd, Pittsburgh PA 15222

The Pittsburgh Convention Center sits on the north bank of the Allegheny River, just minutes from downtown hotels, dining, and the cultural district. The summit occupies the entire third-floor concourse — a 38,000 sq ft space with floor-to-ceiling river views and direct sky-bridge access from the parking garage.

Doors open at 7:30 AM on both October 9 and October 10. Registration and badge pickup are located in the main atrium near Entrance B.

Accessible Transit

  • Light Rail Pittsburgh "T" Allegheny Station — 6 minute walk via the Andy Warhol Bridge.
  • Bus Routes 16, 17, and 54 stop directly on Fort Duquesne Blvd.
  • Airport Pittsburgh International (PIT) — 28 minutes by car or 28X Airport Flyer bus.
  • Parking Convention Center Garage on 10th Street; daily flat rate of $18 for attendees.
  • Accessibility Step-free entrances at all four doors; service-animal-friendly; assistive listening devices available at the help desk.

On-Site Amenities

Every attendee badge includes full access to the third-floor concourse, all breakout spaces, and the riverside terrace lounge. The venue is fully wired with 10 Gbps redundant fiber and offers complimentary high-density Wi-Fi (SSID: strata-2025) across every room.

Catering

Continental breakfast, sit-down lunch, and two coffee/snack breaks each day. Menus include vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and kosher options, all sourced from regional Pennsylvania farms and roasters.

AV & Production

Every keynote and breakout room is fitted with 4K projection, line-array audio, and live captioning. Sessions are recorded and made available to ticket holders within 72 hours of the conference closing.

Breakout Rooms

Six dedicated workshop rooms (capacity 40–120) plus three small "huddle" rooms reservable on demand for ad-hoc 1:1 meetings, hiring conversations, and team syncs throughout the day.

Quiet Lounge

A low-stimulation space on the mezzanine with dimmed lighting, soft seating, and no audio. Open both days for attendees who need a break from the main floor.

Networking Bar

The riverside terrace transforms into a hosted reception each evening from 5:30 to 7:30 PM, featuring local craft beverages and rotating live acoustic acts from Pittsburgh musicians.

Wellness & Care

On-site first aid, a private nursing/parents' room with refrigeration, and an all-gender restroom on every floor. Charging stations and water-bottle refill points are located at all four hallway corners.

Our Mission

Hollow Creek Land Trust conserves the working farms, headwater wetlands, and hardwood ridges that define this corner of the Appalachian range. We work alongside landowners, scientists, and neighbors to keep these places intact for the wildlife and communities that depend on them — today and for generations to come.

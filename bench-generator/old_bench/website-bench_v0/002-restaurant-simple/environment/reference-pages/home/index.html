Trattoria Belluno

— cucina italiana del quartiere —

For more than thirty years, our small dining room on Vine Street has served the recipes our grandmother carried from the foothills of the Dolomites. Every loaf of bread is baked at dawn, every pasta rolled by hand, every sauce simmered slowly through the afternoon. We do not hurry, and we hope you will not either.

Whether you are joining us for a quiet Tuesday supper or gathering the family on a Sunday evening, you will find the same warm welcome, the same honest cooking, and the same bottle of Chianti waiting at the table. Benvenuti — come in, sit down, and stay awhile.

Our monthly giving program

Recurring gifts — as little as $10 a month — give us the steady, reliable footing to negotiate multi-year easements and plan stewardship work seasons in advance. Members of the Hollow Creek Circle receive quarterly field reports and a standing invitation to our autumn gathering in Clay County.

Give Monthly

Our Mission

Hollow Creek Land Trust preserves over 12,000 acres of forested ridgeline and creek-bottom habitat through conservation easements, community partnerships, and volunteer stewardship across the southern Appalachians.

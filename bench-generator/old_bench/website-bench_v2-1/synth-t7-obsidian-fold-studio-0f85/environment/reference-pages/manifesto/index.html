Manifesto · Vol. 07

Shape Before Color.
Silence Before Noise.

Obsidian Fold is a Berlin identity studio building precise, angular visual systems for small institutions, makers, and quiet luxury brands. We treat every mark as architecture — folded, measured, and load-bearing.

Est. 2014 — Berlin · Mitte
§ 01 — Core Beliefs

Three positions we
refuse to compromise.

On Identity

An identity is not a logo. It is the smallest possible set of decisions a brand can make and still remain itself across every surface and decade.

We design systems that can survive being photocopied, embossed, and forgotten — then rediscovered without apology.

A mark earns its place by what it refuses to say.

On Craft

Every curve is drawn twice. Once to find it, once to defend it. Our files are organized like sentences — punctuated, indented, structurally sound.

Pixel hinting matters. Kerning matters. The weight of a counter at 9pt matters more than the gradient on a billboard.

Precision is not a style. It is a posture.

On Restraint

The hardest line to draw is the one you remove. We start every project with subtraction, working downward toward the irreducible.

Restraint is not minimalism. It is editorial — knowing exactly which two notes to play, and exactly how long to hold them.

Silence is a typographic decision.

§ 02 — Practice

A studio of four,
by appointment only.

Slow by design

We accept six to eight engagements per year. Each one runs twelve to twenty weeks, beginning with a week of listening and ending with a printed manual.

Built to fold

Our deliverables are bound, not zipped. We ship a guidelines book, a metal-plated mark, and the source files — in that order of importance.

Quietly opinionated

We do not pitch. We do not present three directions. We arrive with one answer, and we defend it with drawings, references, and proofs.

Obsidian Fold

A Debut Collection — 23 Poems in Three Sections

The Longitude
of Smoke

These poems were written along a river that forgets nothing and admits less. They are a cartography of furnaces, of grandmothers, of the slow erosion between what we inherit and what we are permitted to keep.
Author Céleste Vaudreuil Year MMXXIII Press Ashen Meridian

§ 05 — TRANSPARENCY
EDITOR'S NOTE

An 18-Month Record of How We Looked.

This report was assembled between May 2023 and October 2024 by a six-person editorial team working with three independent data auditors. The objective was narrow: to test, with verifiable federal and state-level records, the persistent industry claim that North America's inland freight rail network is "structurally sound, if aging."

We requested every Class II and Class III bridge inspection report filed with the Federal Railroad Administration between 2009 and 2023. We cross-referenced these against Surface Transportation Board incident logs, state DOT maintenance disbursements, and 62 on-the-record field interviews conducted across eleven states.

What follows is the complete sourcing record. Where redactions were demanded by carriers, we have noted them. Where records were unavailable, we have said so.

La Carte

Automne · Service from 18 h

Amuse-Bouche

· · ·

Offered by the kitchen at the beginning of each service, these one-bite preparations change nightly with the market. Consider them an introduction rather than a course—a quiet handshake before the meal begins.

  • Gougère au Comté — pâte à choux, aged Comté, cracked pepper 6
  • Œuf Mimosa — smoked trout roe, fines herbes, brown butter 8
  • Toast au Beurre Noisette — sourdough, anchoïade, lemon zest 7

Entrées

· · ·

Cold and warm beginnings drawn from the Lyonnaise canon, lightened for the table at hand. Most are intended to be shared, though no one will object if you keep the salade lyonnaise to yourself.

  • Salade Lyonnaise — frisée, lardons, croutons, poached farm egg 16
  • Quenelle de Brochet — pike dumpling, sauce nantua, tarragon 22
  • Terrine de Campagne — pork, chicken liver, cornichons, country bread 18
  • Velouté de Topinambour — sunchoke, hazelnut praline, brown butter 15
“We butcher whole animals on Tuesdays; the menu is never quite the same twice.” — Élise Fournier, Chef

Plats

· · ·

Main courses cooked slowly, plated simply, and meant to be eaten in good company. Substitutions are gracefully declined; the kitchen has reasons for the pairings on the page.

  • Poulet de Bresse Rôti — for two, sauce au vin jaune, morels, pommes purée 86
  • Andouillette à la Ficelle — Dijon mustard cream, frites maison 34
  • Joue de Bœuf Braisée — red wine, root vegetables, gremolata 38
  • Truite au Bleu — Charlevoix trout, court-bouillon, brown butter 32
  • Cardons à la Moelle — cardoon gratin, bone marrow, parmesan 28

Desserts

· · ·

Classics finished à la minute, with one cheese option from our affineur in Saint-Hyacinthe. Coffee and digestifs are listed separately on the cellar card—ask your server.

  • Tarte aux Pralines Roses — Lyonnaise pink praline, crème fraîche 12
  • Île Flottante — meringue, crème anglaise, caramel filé 11
  • Poire Pochée au Burgundy — Anjou pear, mascarpone, black pepper 13
  • Plateau de Fromages — selection of three, walnut bread, honeycomb 19

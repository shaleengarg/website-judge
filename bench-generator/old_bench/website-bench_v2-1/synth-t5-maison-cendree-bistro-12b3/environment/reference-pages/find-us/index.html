Find Us

Between a florist whose window glows green at dusk and a bookshop stacked with French paperbacks, you will find an unmarked black door. There is no sign, only a small brass numeral. Push the door; the dining room waits down a short flight of stairs, candlelit and quiet, exactly where we have kept it since 2011.

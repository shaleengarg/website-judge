KEYNOTE
Opening Keynote · Day One, 9:00 AM

Dr. Margaret Okonkwo-Reyes

Chief Technology Officer, Allegheny Forge Systems

A twenty-year veteran of advanced manufacturing, Dr. Okonkwo-Reyes leads one of the largest re-shored steel and composites operations in the Midwest. Her keynote, "The Quiet Industrial Decade," draws on field data from 140 plant retrofits to argue that the next inflection point in U.S. production will be driven not by automation alone, but by a workforce-software-policy triad most coastal investors continue to underestimate.

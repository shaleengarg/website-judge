Clause 7.2 — Excerpt
Indemnification and Limitation of Liability

The Provider shall, to the fullest extent reasonably practicable, indemnify and hold harmless the Client from any and all consequential damages arising out of material breaches of this Agreement, except where such breach results from the Client's gross negligence or willful misconduct.

Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Provider's aggregate liability shall in no event exceed amounts commensurate with the fees paid during the preceding twelve (12) months, and shall exclude any claims arising under force majeure, subrogation, or third-party tortious interference.

Any dispute shall be resolved in a timely manner through good-faith negotiation before resorting to binding arbitration in the jurisdiction set forth in Schedule A.

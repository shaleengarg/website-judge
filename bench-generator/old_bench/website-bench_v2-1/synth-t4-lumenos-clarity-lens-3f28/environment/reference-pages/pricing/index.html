Common Questions

Everything you need to know before bringing Lumenos to your team.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your workspace settings. Pro-rated credits are applied automatically to your next invoice.

Is my contract data confidential?

Always. All documents are encrypted at rest and in transit, and never used to train shared models. Enterprise customers may opt for private model isolation.

What counts as a "document"?

Any uploaded file up to 200 pages counts as one document. Longer files are automatically split, and revisions of the same contract do not consume new credits.

Do you offer non-profit discounts?

Yes — registered non-profits, legal aid organizations, and academic institutions receive 40% off Professional pricing. Contact our team to verify eligibility.

The Sanctuary Spa

Rituals of the Cove

Carved into the cliffside above a black-sand inlet, our seven signature treatments draw on volcanic minerals, deep-sea botanicals, and a century of Pacific island healing. Each ritual is performed in a candlelit private suite with an open view of the obsidian shore.

I

Volcanic Ash Exfoliation

A full-body polish using mineral-rich ash gathered from the island's northern caldera, blended with cold-pressed coconut oil. Skin emerges luminous, silken, and entirely renewed beneath the warmth of a basalt-stone finish.

75 Minutes$320
II

Black Pearl Facial

Crushed Tahitian black pearl and marine collagen are layered over the skin in a luminous five-stage treatment. The ritual concludes with a cool gold-leaf mask that firms the contours and restores a quiet inner light.

90 Minutes$410
III

Deep Tide Massage

A rhythmic, full-pressure massage choreographed to the cadence of the surf below, releasing tension through long oceanic strokes. Warm kukui-nut oil melts into the muscles while distant tide-song threads through the room.

90 Minutes$360
IV

Obsidian Stone Therapy

Heated volcanic obsidian stones, each hand-polished on the island, are placed along the meridians to draw out deep-set fatigue. The result is a profound stillness — a body grounded, anchored to the earth beneath it.

80 Minutes$385
V

Sea Kelp Wrap

Cold-water kelp and Pacific spirulina are blended into a warm clay cocoon that detoxifies and remineralises the skin. As the wrap softens, a sustained scalp massage carries you into an effortless, weightless calm.

60 Minutes$290
VI

Sulphur Spring Soak

A private immersion in our naturally-fed sulphur pools above the cove, served with chilled jasmine tea and a small plate of seasonal fruit. The mineral waters are believed to release joint tension and quiet the mind.

45 Minutes$220
VII

Midnight Ritual Package

Our most extensive sanctuary journey: an ash exfoliation, deep tide massage, obsidian therapy, and pearl facial performed in sequence beneath the stars. Concludes with champagne service on a private cliff-edge terrace.

4 Hours$1,180

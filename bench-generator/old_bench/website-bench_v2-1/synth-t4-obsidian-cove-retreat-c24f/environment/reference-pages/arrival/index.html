The Elements of Seclusion

Three rare privileges, included with every stay

◐

Private Cove Access

A crescent of volcanic black sand reserved exclusively for resort guests, with hand-shaped tide pools and shaded cabanas curated by a dedicated beach concierge.

♨

Volcanic Hot Springs

Geothermal mineral pools rise from deep beneath the caldera, ranging from 38°C to 42°C and framed by carved obsidian walls under an open sky.

✦

Stargazing Terrace

A cantilevered observatory deck suspended over the Pacific, where an in-house astronomer reads the southern constellations after the last service ends.

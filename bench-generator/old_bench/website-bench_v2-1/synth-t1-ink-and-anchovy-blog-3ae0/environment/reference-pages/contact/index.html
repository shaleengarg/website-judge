Contact

Get in touch

Marta welcomes pitches from food magazines and editors working on Mediterranean, Nordic, or culinary-history subjects. Questions about a particular recipe, a substitution, or a book recommendation are always read — though answers may be slower than the asking.

marta@inkandanchovy.no

Replies usually within two weeks. Please be patient during travel months.

A small but firm note: Ink & Anchovy does not accept advertising, sponsored content, affiliate placements, or paid product mentions of any kind. Press samples and review copies are also politely declined. The blog is, and intends to remain, a quiet personal project.

For postal correspondence regarding manuscripts or longer-form work, please write first and a mailing address will be shared by reply.

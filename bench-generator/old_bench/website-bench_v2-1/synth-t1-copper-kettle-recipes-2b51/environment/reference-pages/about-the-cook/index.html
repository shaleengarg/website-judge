About the Cook

MV
Marta Vásárhelyi

Marta Vásárhelyi grew up in Debrecen, in the paprika-loving heart of eastern Hungary, where Sunday afternoons smelled of slowly rendering lard and sweet onions softening in a battered iron pot. She learned this goulash recipe at her grandmother's elbow, taught to test the meat with a wooden spoon and to never, ever stir the paprika into anything still bubbling.

Today she lives in Toronto with her husband and an unreasonable collection of cast-iron cookware, where she teaches weekend cooking workshops out of a small studio kitchen in the east end. Copper Kettle Recipes is her way of pinning down the single dish she has cooked more times than any other — the one she still calls home.

What You'll Need

A short shopping list, but every item earns its place. Source the best paprika you can find — it's the soul of the pot.

For the Goulash

  • 700g beef chuck, cut into 4cm cubes
  • 3 tbsp pork lard (or neutral oil)
  • 2 large yellow onions, finely diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp sweet Hungarian paprika
  • 1 tsp hot Hungarian paprika (optional)
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or 1 tbsp tomato paste)
  • 2 medium carrots, cut into thick coins
  • 3 medium waxy potatoes, cut into 3cm chunks
  • 1 tsp caraway seeds, lightly crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1.2 L beef stock, warm
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

For Serving

  • Crusty country bread or csipetke (pinched egg noodles)
  • Sour cream, for dolloping
  • Fresh flat-leaf parsley, roughly chopped
  • Pickled cherry peppers, on the side

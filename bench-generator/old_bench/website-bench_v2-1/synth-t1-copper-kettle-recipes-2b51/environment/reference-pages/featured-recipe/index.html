Hungarian Beef Goulash

Smoky sweet paprika perfumes the kitchen as ribbons of softened onion melt into a deep, rust-colored stew. Beneath the gentle bubble of the pot, chunks of chuck braise for hours until they yield to the press of a spoon.

  • Prep Time: 20 min
  • Cook Time: 2 hr 30 min
  • Servings: 6
  • Difficulty: Intermediate

Capabilities

Six lenses on every contract you sign.

Lumenos breaks open dense legal language with focused analytical engines — each tuned to surface a specific class of risk in seconds, not days.

Ambiguity Scanner

Identifies vague modifiers, undefined antecedents, and qualifier drift across every clause. Each finding ships with a precise location and a plain-language rewrite suggestion.

Core Engine

Jargon Density Score

Quantifies the ratio of specialized legal terminology to readable prose on a 0–100 scale. Benchmarks your draft against industry baselines for litigation, M&A, and procurement contracts.

Metrics

Readability Heatmap

Visualizes paragraph-level reading difficulty using Flesch–Kincaid grade overlays. Stakeholders see exactly which sections will lose a non-specialist reader before they sign.

Visualization

Clause Conflict Detector

Cross-references every obligation, term, and exception within the document to flag contradictions. Surfaces conflicts between termination, indemnity, and governing-law clauses automatically.

Risk

Version Diff Clarity

Compares revisions side-by-side and ranks each change by clarity impact, not just text difference. Spot the one redline that quietly broadened liability without scanning two hundred pages.

Collaboration

Multi-Language Audit

Runs the full Lumenos analysis on contracts in English, Spanish, German, French, and Mandarin. Translation-fidelity scoring flags clauses that lose meaning across bilingual master agreements.

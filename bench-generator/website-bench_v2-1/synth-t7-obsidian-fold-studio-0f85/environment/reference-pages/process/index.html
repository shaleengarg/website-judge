Method · 04 movements

Four folds
from question
to finished mark.

Every identity we build passes through the same disciplined sequence — never skipped, never rushed. The work below is how a brand becomes itself in our hands: discovered, distilled, drawn, and deployed.

01
Phase 01 · Weeks 1–2

Discover — we listen until the question is precise.

We interview founders, staff, and skeptics. We audit the category, the competitors, and the ghosts in the room. Nothing is drawn yet. The deliverable of this phase is a single, sharpened brief — usually one paragraph long.

  • Stakeholder interviews 10–14 sessions
  • Category cartography field map
  • Lexicon & tone audit verbatim deck
  • Sharpened brief 1 paragraph
Artifact · 01

The Sharpened Brief

One paragraph. No hedges. Signed by the founders before we touch a pencil. If we cannot agree on this, we do not proceed.

02
Phase 02 · Weeks 3–4

Distill — we reduce until what remains is unbreakable.

Strategy hardens into form-language. We choose three governing words, a geometric posture, and a palette philosophy. Anything that cannot survive being said out loud at room volume is cut.

  • Three governing words core triad
  • Form posture angular / soft / hybrid
  • Palette philosophy 2 anchor hues
  • Reject list what we are not
Artifact · 02

The Reject List

Equally important as what the brand will become. Pinned to the studio wall for the remainder of the engagement — a fence against drift.

03
Phase 03 · Weeks 5–9

Draw — we cut every line by hand, then by grid.

Logomarks begin on paper, migrate to vector, and survive a punishing internal critique. Typography is paired, intervals are measured, and a working identity system is constructed at production fidelity.

  • Logomark routes 3 directions
  • Type pairing display + text
  • Grid & geometry doc angle table
  • Living system file working build
Artifact · 03

The Angle Table

The mathematical spine of the identity. Every rotation, chamfer, and skew in the system is referenced back to this single document.

04
Phase 04 · Weeks 10–12

Deploy — we hand over a system that defends itself.

The identity ships with a guidelines document precise enough to survive a decade of in-house designers. We supervise the first three real-world applications, then step back. The brand belongs to the client now.

  • Guidelines · 80–120 pages PDF + web
  • Asset library SVG · PDF · OTF
  • First three applications supervised
  • One-year stewardship quarterly review
Artifact · 04

The Stewardship Letter

A printed note signed by the studio and the founders, listing the four rules that must never be broken. Framed; usually hung in reception.

Main Halls

Carnegie Hall

Monongahela Room

CAPACITY 180 · MEZZANINE LEVEL

A roundtable-format hall used for breakout panels, technical workshops, and closed-door legislative briefings. Configurable seating with whiteboards, dual projection, and embedded press tables.

  • Format Roundtable, workshop
  • Sessions Panels, Briefings
  • Capacity 180 seats
  • Access Elevator, ADA

October 14 · Day Zero

Pre-Conference Workshops

Hands-on, small-group sessions held on Tuesday, October 14 — the day before the main conference opens. Workshops require separate registration in addition to your IRONCLAD 2025 pass, and seats are limited. Materials, lab access, and lunch are included with every workshop ticket.

Workshop 01 · Morning

Digital Twins for Discrete Manufacturing

FacilitatorDr. Marisol Avendaño
Duration4 hours
CapacityMax 30 attendees

Participants will build a working digital twin of a simulated assembly line, integrating real-time sensor data and predictive analytics. By the end of the session, attendees will be able to identify high-value twin use cases and outline an adoption roadmap for their own plant.

Workshop 02 · Morning

Additive Manufacturing with Metal Alloys

FacilitatorProf. Henrik Bauer
Duration6 hours
CapacityMax 20 attendees

A deep technical dive into laser powder-bed fusion, directed energy deposition, and post-processing for aerospace and automotive grade components. Attendees will work alongside CMU lab engineers to evaluate sample prints and benchmark mechanical performance.

Workshop 03 · Afternoon

Industrial AI: From Pilot to Production

FacilitatorReena Krishnamurthy
Duration4 hours
CapacityMax 40 attendees

Move beyond proof-of-concept by learning the operational patterns that make machine learning sustainable on the factory floor. Participants will leave with a deployment checklist covering MLOps, drift monitoring, and cross-functional governance.

Workshop 04 · Afternoon

Decarbonizing Heavy Industry Operations

FacilitatorThomas Okafor, PE
Duration5 hours
CapacityMax 25 attendees

A scenario-driven workshop on cutting Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions in steel, cement, and chemicals plants without sacrificing throughput. Teams will model a real retrofit case study and present financed pathways to net-zero by 2040.

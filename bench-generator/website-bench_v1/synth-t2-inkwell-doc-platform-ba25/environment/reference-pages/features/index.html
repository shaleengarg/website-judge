Everything Your Team Needs

Inkwell pairs intelligent automation with thoughtful editorial tools so your documentation stays organized, current, and trusted. Each feature is designed to remove friction from the way engineering teams actually write and find knowledge.

AI Tag Suggestions
Feature 01

AI Tag Suggestions

Inkwell reads every new page as it's written and proposes a handful of concise, consistent tags based on your team's existing taxonomy. You can accept, edit, or ignore each suggestion in one click, so tagging never breaks your flow. Over time, the model adapts to your conventions and helps surface related documents your readers would have otherwise missed.

Version History
Feature 02

Version History

Every edit is captured automatically with author, timestamp, and a clean diff view that highlights exactly what changed. Rolling back to any previous revision takes a single click, with no risk of overwriting a teammate's work in progress. Annotated checkpoints let leads mark "approved" or "stable" versions so readers always know which copy is canonical.

Slack Import
Feature 03

Slack Import

Promote a Slack thread into a structured document with one shortcut, preserving participants, code snippets, and links. Inkwell automatically de-duplicates messages, groups follow-ups, and proposes a title and category based on the conversation topic. Tribal knowledge that used to vanish into channel scrollback becomes a permanent, searchable part of your hub.

Role-Based Access
Feature 04

Role-Based Access

Define granular roles like Reader, Editor, Reviewer, and Admin, then apply them at the workspace, space, or individual page level. Permissions inherit cleanly through your folder structure, so onboarding a new squad takes minutes instead of an afternoon of audit work. SSO and SCIM provisioning keep access in sync with your identity provider as people move between teams.

Get in Touch

I welcome notes from readers — about books you think I should know, quiet recommendations, translations you've been working on, or simply something that struck you on a long walk. I also read every message about writing commissions, copywriting work, and editorial collaborations, though I may take a few sleepless evenings to reply.

Please note that I do not accept sponsored posts, affiliate placements, or paid link insertions of any kind. Ink & Insomnia is a personal blog, and I'd like to keep it that way. Pitches that ignore this will be quietly archived.

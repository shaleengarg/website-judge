Reading the Donut

As of Q1 2024, the GEBCO/Seabed 2030 initiative has produced bathymetric coverage of roughly one-fifth of Earth's ocean floor at modern resolution (≥800m grid). The remaining 79.1% remains charted only by satellite altimetry-derived gravity inference.

  • Modern bathymetry 20.9%
  • Altimetry only 79.1%
  • Target 2030 100%

About

I am a designer based in Lisbon, working independently since 2018. Before that I spent six years at studios in Amsterdam and Berlin, where I learned that most of the work happens before anything is drawn. My practice covers identity, editorial, and digital product — though I think of these as the same activity in different materials.

I take on a small number of projects each year, usually for publishers, cultural institutions, and people starting something on their own. I prefer long conversations to short briefs, and I am happy to work at a distance. If you think we might be a good fit, write to me at iris@mendel.studio.

About the Summit

Forging the Future of Industrial Resilience.

Since 2018, the Ironclad Futures Summit has gathered North America's most influential operations directors, logistics engineers, and policy makers in Pittsburgh to confront the hardest problems facing modern manufacturing and supply chains.

Our Mission

The Ironclad Futures Summit was founded in 2018 by the Ironclad Industry Alliance, a non-profit consortium of mid-market manufacturers, regional logistics operators, and academic researchers committed to strengthening the industrial backbone of North America.

We exist to connect the people who actually move steel, semiconductors, and shipments — operations leaders, port authorities, customs specialists, plant managers, and the policy makers who shape the rules they operate under. Our programming prioritizes hard-won operational insight over theory.

Over six prior summits we have welcomed more than 4,200 alumni from 38 states and 9 countries, and seeded working groups that continue to publish open standards on supplier diversification, near-shoring, and dual-source resilience.

"Resilience is not a product you buy — it is a discipline you build, together, in advance of the disruption."

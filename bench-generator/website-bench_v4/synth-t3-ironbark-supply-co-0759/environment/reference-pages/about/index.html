Our Story

Built for the Bush Since 2009

A workshop in the Central Highlands — sawdust, hot steel, and hickory shavings.

Founded in Ballarat, Victoria

Ironbark Supply Co. began in 2009 in a corrugated-iron shed on the outskirts of Ballarat, where founder Dale Ferrington spent his weekends re-handling old axes pulled from farm clearing-sales. What started as a one-man restoration habit grew, slowly and stubbornly, into a small workshop turning out tools for shearers, fencers, hikers and weekend bush-walkers across the Central Highlands.

Fifteen years on, we still operate from a workshop in Ballarat — larger now, with a forge, a leather bench, and a canvas room — but the brief has not changed. Build gear that earns its keep. Use materials you can name. Stand behind every piece that leaves the door.

We are a team of nine. We sharpen what we ship. We answer the phone when it rings.

Caring for Your Hide

Store flat or loosely rolled in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and stiffening.

  • Condition every 6–12 months with a neutral leather balm.
  • Blot spills immediately; never soak.
  • Avoid plastic wrap — leather needs to breathe.
  • Test any finish or dye on a scrap before applying broadly.

Shipping & Returns

Hides ship rolled in protective kraft within 1–2 business days from our Portland workshop. Domestic ground delivery: 3–6 business days. International orders ship via USPS Priority and may incur customs duties.

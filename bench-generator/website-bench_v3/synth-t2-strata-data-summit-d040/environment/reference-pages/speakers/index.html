Speakers

Eight voices shaping the future of data engineering and analytics.

Our 2025 lineup features Dr. Amara Okonkwo, Liang Chen, Marisol Vega, Devon Pritchard, Yuki Tanaka, Rohan Mehta, Cara Whitfield, and Ibrahim Al-Sayed — eight confirmed practitioners and leaders joining us in Pittsburgh on October 9–10. Expect candid keynotes, working-session masterclasses, and informal lounge conversations across two full days.

AO
Dr. Amara Okonkwo
Chief Data Officer
Helix Insurance Group
Amara leads a 200-person data org modernizing decades of legacy actuarial pipelines. She has spent the last fifteen years translating regulated-industry constraints into pragmatic platform decisions.
LC
Liang Chen
Principal Data Engineer
Northwind Logistics
Liang designs streaming systems that move billions of telemetry events from trucks and warehouses every day. He writes openly about the trade-offs between batch warehousing and real-time architectures.
MV
Marisol Vega
VP of Analytics
Beacon Health Partners
Marisol builds analytics functions inside hospital networks where data quality directly affects patient outcomes. Her keynote will cover governance frameworks that survive contact with operational reality.
DP
Devon Pritchard
Staff ML Engineer
Cartograph AI
Devon ships production machine-learning systems for geospatial routing and demand prediction. He is leading a hands-on workshop on shipping reproducible feature stores without a small army of platform engineers.
YT
Yuki Tanaka
Head of Data Platform
Lumen Retail
Yuki rebuilt Lumen's analytics stack from scratch over eighteen months, consolidating six warehouses into one. She'll share what worked, what she'd do differently, and the migration patterns that scaled.
RM
Rohan Mehta
Director of Data Science
Greenfield Energy
Rohan brings physics-informed modeling to grid optimization across the U.S. Northeast. His session unpacks how to evaluate forecasting models when the cost of being wrong is measured in megawatts.
CW
Cara Whitfield
Senior Analytics Engineer
Mosaic Media
Cara is a longtime advocate of the analytics-engineering discipline and a contributor to several open-source modeling tools. She'll walk through patterns for keeping semantic layers honest as teams scale.
IA
Ibrahim Al-Sayed
Founder & CEO
Parallax Data Labs
Ibrahim founded Parallax after a decade leading data teams at consumer fintech companies. His closing keynote looks at where the data tooling market is consolidating — and where new gaps are quietly opening.

Features

Every Tool A Modern Writer Actually Needs.

Luminos combines six precision-engineered capabilities into one fluid copilot — so your first draft is closer to your final draft.

✺
Drafting

Contextual Drafting

Luminos reads the surrounding paragraphs, your reference docs, and your style notes before suggesting a single word. Every output stays anchored to your subject — not generic AI filler.

◐
Tone

Tone Calibration

Dial sentences between executive, casual, persuasive, or technical with a single slider. Luminos rewrites in place while preserving meaning and structure across the entire passage.

❖
Sources

Citation Weaving

Drop in PDFs, links, or pasted excerpts and Luminos quotes them inline with footnoted attributions. Hover any sentence to trace its origin back to the source paragraph.

⏱
Speed

Deadline Mode

Set a target word count and a finish time — Luminos prioritizes momentum over polish, generating skeleton sections first. A focus timer keeps revisions on track until you ship.

⚿
Voice

Brand Voice Lock

Train a private voice profile from five sample documents and Luminos will never drift outside it. Lock vocabulary, sentence length, and forbidden phrases at the workspace level.

⟁
Global

Multi-Language Spin

Translate, transcreate, and localize across 32 languages without losing tone. Idioms, units, and regional nuances are adapted automatically — not literally re-typed word for word.

Features

Everything your team needs to ship words at the speed of code.

Luminos plugs into the tools you already use and turns the messy reality of product work — half-finished Notion docs, cryptic Jira tickets, scattered Slack threads — into polished, customer-ready writing in seconds.

Auto-Draft from Jira

Point Luminos at an epic or a sprint label and it stitches every ticket, commit, and PR into a coherent release note draft.

No more copy-pasting summaries — the customer-facing prose writes itself, with proper grouping by feature, fix, and improvement.

Tone Calibration Sliders

Dial the same draft from terse engineering changelog to upbeat marketing email with three sliders: formality, hype, and technical depth.

One-Click Confluence Publish

Approved draft? Push it directly to Confluence, Notion, your help center, or a scheduled customer email — formatting and links intact.

Version history syncs back, so future releases reuse the same structure and your docs stay in lockstep with shipping reality.

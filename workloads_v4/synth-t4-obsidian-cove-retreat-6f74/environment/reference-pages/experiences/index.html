Curated Coastal Experiences

The Aegean,
On Your Terms

Five hand-crafted encounters with the sea, the cliffs, and the artisans who have shaped this coastline for centuries. Each guided by a single host, limited to a single party.

A Private Itinerary, Quietly Composed

Reserve any experience individually, or weave them together into a multi-day arc with our concierge. All experiences include transfers, refreshments, and a take-home memento.

01 · Dawn
Sunrise · 2 Hours

Aegean Kayak at Dawn

Slip into translucent water as the sun lifts over the headland. Your guide, a third-generation islander, leads a slow paddle through sea-arches and hidden coves only reachable by water — returning to a thermos of Greek mountain tea on the pebbled shore.

Skill: Beginner Max 4 Guests €140 / person
Reserve
02 · Stillness
Morning · 75 Minutes

Clifftop Yoga with Ioannis

On a limestone terrace 80 metres above the waves, Ioannis — formerly of an Athens monastic school — guides a slow Hatha practice timed to the wind. Mats, linen bolsters, and chilled eucalyptus towels provided. Suitable for all levels.

All Levels Max 8 Guests €85 / person
Reserve
03 · Craft
Afternoon · 3 Hours

Octopus Ink Pottery Workshop

In a whitewashed studio inland, the ceramicist Maria-Eleni demonstrates an ancient pigment technique using cuttlefish ink and volcanic ash. Throw a small vessel on the wheel, glaze it, and we will fire and ship the finished piece to your home within six weeks.

No Experience Needed Max 6 Guests €220 / person
Reserve
04 · Voyage
Full Day · 9 Hours

Private Sailing to Naxos

Board a restored 14-metre wooden ketch with a two-person crew. Sail north along the Cycladic chain, anchor for a long lunch of grilled fish and chilled assyrtiko in a marble-pebbled bay, and return under the lighthouse-lit dusk. Up to six guests.

Lunch Included Max 6 Guests €1,480 / charter
Reserve
05 · Dusk
Golden Hour · 2.5 Hours

Dusk Spearfishing Lesson

Captain Stelios — former Hellenic freediving champion — leads a small group into the shallow reef as the light turns amber. Learn breath-hold technique, traditional sling-spear handling, and ethical harvesting. Your catch is cleaned, grilled, and served back at the cove.

Intermediate Swimmer Max 3 Guests €260 / person
Reserve

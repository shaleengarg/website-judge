Catalog Note 01

Volumes 01–04 are out of print. Library copies held at the Bibliothèque Forney (Paris), Letterform Archive (San Francisco), and the Bauhaus-Archiv (Berlin).

Catalog Note 02

A scanning project for volumes 01–06 was completed in 2022. Digital facsimiles are accessible to institutional subscribers via the Forma Negra reading-room portal.

Catalog Note 03

Volume 09 (Silence, 2020) was the only edition published outside the biannual rhythm, released as a slim 64-page foldout under pandemic conditions.

From the Editor

A Letter Filed Against Time

When we shipped the first folded sheet of Chlorophyll Dispatch from a leaking pressroom in Galway in the spring of 2020, we did not yet know whether long-form environmental journalism still had a constituency. Forty issues later, the archive you are reading below is the answer: a slow, deliberate ledger of bog water and river chemistry, of land disputes settled by patience and unsettled by drought, of correspondents who learned to file copy from places without addresses. We have re-indexed each issue here for the working researcher and the curious reader alike — every cover story, every featured dispatch, every dateline. Eight of these entries are marked as Editor's Selection: pieces we still send to new staff on their first day. The rest are sequenced strictly by month of publication. Read backwards into our weather; read forwards into our argument. Either way, the chlorophyll keeps moving.

— M. Halloran, Editor-in-Chief · Connemara, December 2024

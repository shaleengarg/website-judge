Voices of the Summit

Speakers & Practitioners

Our speaker roster is curated by working engineers, not committees. We prioritize practitioner experience over academic credentials: every talk this year comes from someone who has shipped, broken, and rebuilt the systems they're describing. Expect concrete war stories, postmortems, and architectures you can take home on Monday.

Keynote Spotlight

DH
Day 1 Opening Keynote · Wed Oct 9, 9:00 AM

Dana Halverson

Principal Site Reliability Engineer · GridForge
Designing for Failure at Scale

After a decade running multi-region infrastructure, Dana argues that "resilience" is a misleading word — what we actually want is graceful degradation under conditions nobody designed for. This keynote walks through three real incidents and the architectural patterns that turned each one into a non-event by the second year.

Keynote   Hall A — Main Stage  ·  50 min + Q&A

JT
Day 2 Opening Keynote · Thu Oct 10, 9:00 AM

Jian Tao

Distinguished Engineer · CoreStack
The Hidden Cost of Eventual Consistency

Eventual consistency is the default escape hatch for distributed systems — but the bill arrives slowly, in support tickets, reconciliation jobs, and 3 AM pages. Jian unpacks the operational debt that compounds quietly behind "AP" systems, and offers a decision framework for when stronger guarantees are worth the latency.

Keynote   Hall A — Main Stage  ·  50 min + Q&A

David L. Lawrence Convention Center

Set on the banks of the Allegheny River, the Lawrence Center is a LEED-certified, light-filled venue with 313,000 sq ft of contiguous exhibit space — purpose-built for technical conferences like ours.

Parking

Convention Center Garage — Open 24/7

The attached underground garage offers 700 spaces with direct elevator access to Level 1 (Registration). Daily rate: $22 flat with conference validation at the registration desk. Standard non-validated rate is $32/day. EV chargers (J1772 and CCS) are available on levels P2 and P3 on a first-come basis. Overflow parking at the Grant Street Garage (5-minute walk) runs $18/day.

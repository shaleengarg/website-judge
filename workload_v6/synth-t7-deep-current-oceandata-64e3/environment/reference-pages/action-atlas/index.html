§ 03 / Policy Timeline

Forty Years of Ocean Law

FIG 3.1

Milestone Treaties & Declarations · 1982 → 2023

8 EVENTS · 41 YEAR SPAN
UNCLOS (1982) established the territorial framework. BBNJ (2023) closed the high-seas governance gap, allowing protected designations beyond national jurisdiction for the first time.

UNCLOS · 1982

EIF 1994

Defined EEZs, continental shelves, and the "common heritage" principle for the deep seabed. 168 parties.

BBNJ High Seas Treaty · 2023

RATIFICATION ONGOING

First legal mechanism to create MPAs in the 64% of ocean beyond national jurisdiction. Needs 60 ratifications to enter force.

Brand Architecture

from $48,000

Structural work for institutions whose offer has outgrown a single mark. We map portfolios, clarify endorsement logic, and write naming systems that scale across exhibitions, research labs, and public programmes.

Typical deliverables

Portfolio audit, taxonomy diagram, naming framework, sub-brand guidelines, governance playbook, two stakeholder workshops.

Duration 10–14 wks Team 4 people Category Strategy

Editorial Design

from $22,000

Long-form publications for museums, research consortia, and university presses. We pair narrative pacing with rigorous typographic systems suited to dense citation work and image-led essays.

Typical deliverables

Editorial grid, type specimen, master InDesign template, cover system, print specification, two rounds of layout production.

Duration 6–12 wks Team 3 people Category Print

Environmental Graphics

from $65,000

Wayfinding, donor recognition, and exhibition signage for sites where the audience is already moving. Designed in close collaboration with architects, fabricators, and curatorial teams.

Typical deliverables

Sign family schedule, location plans, material specifications, ADA compliance audit, fabricator-ready artwork, on-site review.

Duration 14–22 wks Team 5 people Category Spatial

UX Audit

from $14,000

Heuristic and task-based review of existing digital products with specific attention to information architecture, content density, and the institutional voice. Concludes with prioritised intervention roadmap.

Typical deliverables

Heuristic scorecard, journey diagrams, annotated screen tear-downs, prioritised backlog, executive summary, 60-minute readout.

Duration 4–6 wks Team 2 people Category Digital

Motion Identity

from $28,000

Kinetic logo behaviour, transitions, and broadcast packaging extending a static identity into film, screen, and live event contexts. We deliver both finished assets and the rules that govern their reuse.

Typical deliverables

Motion principles document, logo animations, lower thirds, transition library, sound design brief, After Effects source files.

Duration 8–10 wks Team 3 people Category Motion

Content Strategy

from $32,000

Editorial frameworks for institutions that publish constantly but rarely deliberately. We define voice, governance, and the editorial calendar that connects exhibitions, research outputs, and audience programmes.

Typical deliverables

Voice and tone guide, content model, editorial calendar, channel matrix, governance handbook, writers' style sheet.

Duration 8–12 wks Team 3 people Category Strategy

Accessibility Review

from $11,000

WCAG 2.2 AA conformance review across digital and physical touchpoints, paired with practical remediation guidance. We work from lived-experience testing as well as automated tooling.

Typical deliverables

Conformance report, severity-scored issue log, remediation patterns, accessible component examples, team training session.

Duration 3–5 wks Team 2 people Category Digital

Research Synthesis

from $18,000

Mid-engagement work that translates interview transcripts, analytics, and stakeholder interviews into a usable shared model. The output is a working artefact, not a deck — designed to be argued over.

Typical deliverables

Insight clusters, opportunity map, persona snapshots, evidence appendix, hypotheses register, 90-minute synthesis workshop.

Duration 5–7 wks Team 3 people Category Research

Common Questions

Frequently asked

Eight things buyers usually want to know before they switch their signal stack to Lumivex.

What counts as a "signal"?
A signal is any behavioral, firmographic, or relational event we surface about a target account — a hiring spike, a leadership change, a tech-stack swap, intent-data spikes, fundraising news, or a warm-intro path opening up. Each tracked signal is deduped per account per 24-hour window, so your quota reflects real opportunities, not noise.
Can I switch between Monthly and Annual later?
Yes. You can switch billing cycles from the Workspace Settings → Billing page at any time. Switching to annual immediately applies the 20% discount and prorates your remaining monthly balance. Switching back to monthly takes effect at the end of your current annual term.
Do you offer a free trial on Growth?
Every Growth signup includes a 14-day full-feature trial with up to 2,000 tracked signals. No credit card is required to start; we ask for billing details on day 14 if you want to continue. Unused signal credits do not roll over.
Which CRMs and tools do you integrate with?
Native bi-directional sync ships with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and Attio. We push to Slack, Microsoft Teams, Gmail, and Outreach, and pull from Snowflake, BigQuery, and any REST endpoint via our Audience webhooks. Enterprise customers can request a custom connector during onboarding.
How is "tracked signals" counted?
One signal = one qualifying event for one account in a 24-hour window. If three buyers at the same account change roles on the same day, that's one signal. Signals that fail your ICP threshold are never charged. You can see live consumption and a forecast in your Workspace dashboard.
What happens if I exceed my signal quota?
You'll see in-app warnings at 80% and 95% of your monthly quota. We never auto-charge overages on Starter or Growth — instead, alerts pause until the next billing cycle, or you can upgrade with one click. Enterprise customers configure either soft caps or burst capacity in their contract.
Is Lumivex SOC 2 compliant?
Yes. Lumivex is SOC 2 Type II audited, GDPR-aligned, and offers a DPA, sub-processor list, and security questionnaire on request. Enterprise contracts include US or EU data residency, customer-managed encryption keys, and quarterly penetration test summaries.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Monthly plans cancel at the end of the current billing period with one click; you keep access until the period ends. Annual plans can be cancelled or downgraded at renewal. We don't lock data — every account can export their signal history and audience definitions as JSON or CSV at any time.
